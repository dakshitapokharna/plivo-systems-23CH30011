Design

Strategy   Proactive redundancy — no retransmit.

Every packet carries the current primary frame plus a redundant copy of a frame sent d_offset frames earlier. A single burst only causes a miss if it spans both the primary and its redundant copy's packet, so maximising d_offset directly buys burst tolerance.

Formula: d_offset = (delay_ms − 70) / 20  →  at 200 ms: d_offset = 6 frames

This separates primary and redundant by 6 packets — enough to survive the worst burst in Profile C_burst (max burst length = 6).

Bandwidth: Sender tracks a running sent / raw ratio and skips the redundant copy whenever adding it would exceed 1.97×, keeping overhead under the 2.0× cap.
